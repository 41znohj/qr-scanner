📷 How to Read a QR Code

  1. Take a photo of the QR code you want to scan (use your phone's camera)
  2. Crop the image so it only shows the QR code (optional but recommended)
  3. Click "Upload QR Image" below
  4. Choose the photo from your device
💡 Tip: For best results, make sure the QR code is clear, well-lit, and not blurry. Cropping to show only the QR code improves detection accuracy.
